Controls and Info

An Intergalactic Adventure

The aim of the game is simple. Survive for as long as you can. You will recieve a score and a ranking based on your kills, collectables and time survived at the end of the game.

Controls

Left Arrow to move left.

Right Arrow to move right.

Spacebar to jump.

W to fire up.

A to fire left.

S to fire down.

D to fire right.
